Troubleshooting Guide

High Variability in Delivered Volume

  • Check for air bubbles in the collection vessel
  • Verify consistent activation technique
  • Ensure temperature equilibration of test units
  • Validate density measurement of the solution
  • Check for leakage around needle insertion point

Inconsistent Needle Extension

  • Verify calibration of measurement system
  • Check for mechanical interference in the fixture
  • Ensure consistent positioning in the test fixture
  • Examine for component variability in the needle shield
  • Verify proper activation force application

Needle Damage During Integrity Testing

  • Verify material properties match test protocol
  • Check for proper alignment in the test fixture
  • Ensure consistent material tension/mounting
  • Examine for pre-existing needle defects
  • Verify proper activation technique
